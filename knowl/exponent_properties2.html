Fundamental Properties 1.3.4. Zero, Negative, and Fractional Exponents.

  1. \(a^0 = 1\) when \(a\neq 0\text{.}\)
  2. \(a^{-n} = \frac{1}{a^n}\text{:}\) negative exponents denote division.
  3. \(a^{\frac{m}{n}} = \sqrt[n]{a^m} = (\sqrt[n]{a})^m\text{:}\) denominators in fractional exponents denote roots.
