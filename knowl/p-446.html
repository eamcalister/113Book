S4:
multiply \(f(x)\) by \(2.\)
S5:
subtract \(1\) from S4.
Here we think of \(f(x)\) as being enclosed in parentheses and simplify:
\begin{align*} 2f(x) - 1 \amp = \amp 2(3x^2-1) -1 \\ \amp = \amp 6x^2-2-1 \\ \amp = \amp 6x^2-3. \end{align*}
