  1. Linear Functions - These are functions of the form \(y = f(x) = mx + b\text{,}\) where \(m\) and \(b\) are constants (numbers).
  2. Quadratic Functions - These are functions of the form \(y = q(x) = ax^2+bx+c\text{,}\) where \(a\text{,}\) \(b\text{,}\) and \(c\) are constants.
  3. Exponential Functions - These are functions of the form \(y = p(x) = ab^{x}\text{,}\) where \(a\) and \(b\) are constants.
  4. Power Functions - These are functions of the form \(y = q(x) = ax^{p}\text{,}\) where \(a\) and \(p\) are constants.
