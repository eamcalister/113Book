Function Building Procedure 5.2.12.

To find the vertex form, proceed as follows:
  1. Write down standard form equal to the proposed form with \(h\) and \(k\text{.}\)
    \begin{equation*} ax^2 + bx+c = a(x-h)^2 + k \end{equation*}
  2. Expand and collect like terms on the right-hand side.
    \begin{equation*} ax^2+ bx + c = ax^2 - 2ha x + ah^2 + k \end{equation*}
  3. Match coefficients of powers of \(x\) on the left- and right-hand sides to obtain a system of equations to solve for \(h\) and \(k\text{.}\)
    \begin{equation*} {\color{red}a} x^2+ {\color{blue}b}x + {\color{green}c} = {\color{red}a}x^2 {\color{blue}- 2ha }x + {\color{green}ah^2 + k} \end{equation*}
